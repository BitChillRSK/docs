### Adding Rootstock to MetaMask

Use Chainlist for easy setup: [chainlist.org](https://chainlist.org/?search=rootstock)

Or manually add:
```
Network: Rootstock Mainnet
RPC: https://public-node.rsk.co
Chain ID: 30
Symbol: RBTC
Explorer: https://explorer.rsk.co
```
